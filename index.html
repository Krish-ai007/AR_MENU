Setup Guide
1

Create your restaurants

Go to the Restaurants tab → tap + Add. Give each restaurant a name, emoji, and colour. Each restaurant is isolated — its dishes and QR codes are separate.

2

Select active restaurant

Tap Select on any restaurant card. The top banner shows which restaurant is active. All dishes you add will belong to it.

3

Shoot on a black background

Place the dish on a turntable with matte black cloth. Rotate slowly 360° — 8 to 12 seconds. Upload to Cloudinary (free) and copy the .mp4 URL.

4

Add dish to active restaurant

Tap Add tab → fill in name, price, URL → Save. Repeat for all 50+ dishes per restaurant.

5

Print individual QR codes

Each dish has its own QR. Tap QR Code → download → print and place on the table. Customers scan → the dish appears in AR on their table, tagged with the restaurant name.

✦ Multi-Restaurant Tips

🏪 Switch restaurants anytime — the active restaurant banner shows which one is selected
🎨 Each restaurant gets its own accent colour in the AR viewer
📋 Use the Menu tab to verify you're editing the right restaurant before adding dishes
🔢 No limit on restaurants or dishes per restaurant

⚡ CORS Note

Cloudinary → Settings → Security → Allowed Origins → add your website domain. Local files always work immediately for preview.
