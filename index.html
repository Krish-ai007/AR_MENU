How it works

1

Shoot 360° video

Place dish on a cake turntable or walk around it. Record 8–12 sec on phone.

2

Upload to Cloudinary

Free account at cloudinary.com → upload .mp4 → copy the URL ending in .mp4

3

Add item + paste URL

Fill in name, price, paste Cloudinary URL. Save.

4

Download QR → print

Click "Get QR" → Download PNG → print and stick next to dish on menu card.

5

Customer scans QR

Phone asks for camera permission → they allow → dish appears floating on live camera view. They swipe to rotate it in AR!

AR mode — how the background removal works

The app uses the phone's rear camera as the background. Your 360° dish video plays on top with its dark background blended out (mix-blend-mode: screen / lighten). For best results, shoot your dish video on a pure black background — a black cloth or black cardboard works perfectly. The darker the background, the more invisible it becomes over the camera feed.
