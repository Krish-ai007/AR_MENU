How it works

1

Shoot on a solid background

Place dish on a turntable. Use black cloth, white paper, or green screen behind it. Rotate slowly 360°, record 8–12 sec.

2

Upload to Cloudinary

cloudinary.com (free) → Upload → copy the .mp4 URL.

3

Add item here

Paste URL, enter name, price. Select which background you used. Save.

4

Get QR → print

Click "Get QR" → Download PNG → print for the table.

5

Customer scans → dish on their table

Camera opens. The dish appears floating on their real table with background removed. Drag to spin 360°.

Best background for shooting

🟢 Green screen — best results, cleanest edges
⚫ Black cloth — great for most food
⚪ White paper/wall — works for dark-coloured food

In the AR viewer, use the BG buttons + slider to fine-tune the cutout live.
