How It Works
1

Shoot on black background

Place dish on a turntable with matte black cloth behind. Light from above/sides. Rotate slowly 360°, record 8–12 seconds.

2

Upload to Cloudinary

cloudinary.com (free) → Upload → copy .mp4 URL. Add your domain to Allowed Origins in Settings → Security.

3

Add item here

Paste the URL, fill in name, price, ingredients and tags. Save.

4

Get QR → print on table

Click "Get QR" → Download PNG → print and place on the table.

5

Customer scans → dish appears

Drag to rotate 360°. Pinch to resize. Tap the bottom sheet for full details and to add to order.

✦ Shooting Tips

⚫ Matte black cloth — avoid shiny surfaces
💡 Light from above/sides, keep bg dark
📐 Separate dish from cloth edge by 5–10cm
🎥 Slow steady rotation, 10+ seconds minimum

⚡ CORS Note

Background removal reads pixel data from the video. Fix: Cloudinary → Settings → Security → Allowed Origins → add your domain. Local files work immediately.
