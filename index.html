How to use this system

1

Shoot your 360° video

Place the dish on a cake turntable or walk around it in a circle. Record 8–12 seconds of smooth rotation on your phone.

2

Upload video to a free host

Upload the .mp4 to Google Drive, Cloudinary (free tier), or GitHub. Set it to public and copy the direct link.

3

Add item here

Fill in the name, price, and paste the public video URL. Save the item.

4

Generate & download QR

Click "Get QR" on the item card. Download the QR image and print it.

5

Stick QR on physical menu

Put the QR sticker next to the dish name on your menu card. Customer scans → sees 360° viewer on their phone.

6

Host this HTML file

Upload this file to GitHub Pages or Vercel (both free). Use that hosted URL when generating QR codes — it's the viewer page customers open.

Free hosting options

GitHub Pages — upload this file to a repo, enable Pages → free URL
Vercel / Netlify — drag-and-drop this file → instant free URL
