How it works

1

Shoot 360° video

Place dish on a cake turntable or walk around it. Record 8–12 sec on phone.

2

Upload to Cloudinary

Free account at cloudinary.com → upload .mp4 → copy the URL ending in .mp4

3

Add item + paste URL

Fill in name, price, paste Cloudinary URL. Save.

4

Download QR → print

Click "Get QR" → Download PNG → print and stick next to dish on menu card.

5

Customer scans QR

Phone asks for camera permission → they allow → dish appears floating on live camera view. Swipe to rotate!

Tips for best AR results

• Black background mode: shoot on pure black cloth — use the BG Removal slider to dial in threshold.
• White/light background: shoot on white, flip to "Light BG" mode in the AR viewer.
• PNG cutout: use a pre-cut image (no background) as a still — use "Still Image" mode.
• For rotation, shoot a slow turntable video — dragging scrubs through frames for 360° feel.
