How it works

1

Shoot on black background

Place dish on a turntable with black cloth behind and underneath. Good lighting on the dish. Rotate slowly 360°, record 8–12 sec.

2

Upload to Cloudinary

cloudinary.com (free) → Upload → copy the .mp4 URL. Set CORS allowed origin to your domain in Cloudinary settings.

3

Add item here

Paste URL, enter name and price. Save.

4

Get QR → print

Click "Get QR" → Download PNG → print for the table.

5

Customer scans → dish appears in AR

Camera opens. The dish floats on the real table. Drag to spin 360°. Use the Cut slider to fine-tune edge if needed.

Tips for best black background removal

⚫ Use matte black cloth — avoid shiny surfaces that reflect light
💡 Light the dish from above/sides, keep background dark
📐 Keep dish well separated from the background cloth
🎚 In the AR viewer, use the Cut slider to fine-tune edges

📷

See this dish in AR

Point your camera at your table — the dish will appear right on it!
