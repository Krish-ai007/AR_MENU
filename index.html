How it works

1

Shoot on black background

Place dish on a turntable with black matte cloth behind. Light the dish well from above/sides. Rotate slowly 360°, record 8–12 sec.

2

Upload to Cloudinary

cloudinary.com (free) → Upload → copy .mp4 URL. Enable CORS for your domain in Cloudinary settings.

3

Add item here

Paste URL, fill in name, price, ingredients, tags. Save.

4

Get QR → print on table

"Get QR" → Download PNG → print.

5

Customer scans → dish on table

App detects the flat surface, places the dish on it. Drag = rotate 360°. Pinch = resize. Tap info = ingredients + tags.

Tips for perfect black background removal

⚫ Matte black cloth — avoid shiny surfaces
💡 Light from above/sides, keep bg dark
📐 Separate dish from cloth by 5–10cm
🎥 Slow steady rotation, 10+ seconds
