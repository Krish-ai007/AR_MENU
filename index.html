How it works

1

Shoot 360° video on black background

Put dish on a cake turntable. Put a black cloth behind it. Record 8–12 sec slowly rotating.

2

Upload to Cloudinary

cloudinary.com (free) → upload → copy the .mp4 URL.

3

Add item here

Paste URL, name, price. Save.

4

Get QR → print

Click "Get QR" on your item → Download → print for the table.

5

Customer scans → sees dish in AR

Camera opens, dish floats over their table. Drag left/right to spin 360°.

Blend mode tips

Screen (default) — best for black background videos. Dark pixels vanish.
Normal — shows full video with circular crop. Good for any background.
Lighten — softer dark-BG removal, keeps more midtones.
Use Circle / Square shape to switch the crop.
